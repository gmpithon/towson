Transportation

Air Freight

Imported goods coming by air are handled with utmost care noting that most of these goods are fragile. In addition, we arrange for the exportation of the various goods covering Eastern and Cenral Africa region via airstrips and airports present.

Rail Transport

In conjuction with the Railway Corporations in our regions of operation we are proud to provide transportation of a wide array of bulky goods including oil products, timber, charcoal, chemicals etc via the robust railway network inherent in the region.

Road Transport

Towson Trading Company possess diversified machinery to handle any kind of goods to be transported on road. Eastern and Central Africa having good coverage of tarmac roads our esteemed customers are assured of timely delivery of the goods.

Ship Freight

We receive goods from ships plying international waters at the various Eastern African ports and transport them to the various destinations for you. You are assured of upto date information about the progreess of your goods anywhere.
