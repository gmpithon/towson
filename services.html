Warehousing and Supply

Warehousing : The right storage for a wide range of products

Towson is a specialist for warehousing, i.e. professional storage management. For industry-specific products such as textiles, hazardous materials, pharmaceuticals we offer the right concept for each product in specially equipped warehouses and intelligent strategies for handling incoming goods, order picking and outgoing shipments. We also take care of inventory processing, manage return shipments and perform any value added services desired.

Our services range from managing Just In Time process management of raw material through partnership with our customer’s suppliers, to value-adding pre-retail services such as packing, kitting, sequencing, and on-site inventory management at our customers’ facilities to integrate with their distribution chains.

Your Benefits :

  • Higher quality products and perfect service, for example through higher safety standards, high planning reliability and a transparent supply chain
  • Reduced costs, for example through lower investment expenditure, reduced inventory levels, optimum use of synergies
  • Greater success through satisfied customers and contracting parties

Supply : Fast, Reliable supply of products

Towson has an extensive based area of manufacturers in the region and thus offers the best supply service known in our operating region. Products ranging from food commodities, fuel, medication, clothes, pharmaceuticals and other liquid and solid products.

Whether local or an international customer our supply will be shipped to your location from our bonded warehouse in port Mombasa, Kenya the busiest port in Eastern Africa.

What We DO:-
Careful analysis: We closely examine your supply chain, inspecting each and every step, which we then optimise.
Intelligent planning: We align all the players involved, identifying a solution based on what your suppliers are capable of and what your customers require.
Reliable coordination: We optimise the interplay of your goods and information streams – "just in time" or exact delivery at the point of sale.
Transparent monitoring: We create IT interfaces in order to harmonise your systems and connect all the players around the world, who can then seamlessly follow what is happening along the entire chain of transportation and across all continents and modes of transport. The result: Discrepancies and bottlenecks can be identified at an early stage and remedied.
