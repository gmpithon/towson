Who We Are

Towson Trading Company Ltd. (TTC Ltd.) is a One-stop Outfit for Transport, Supply Services and Construction Industries .We are registered as a Private Limited Liability Company under the Laws of the Republic of Kenya to undertake Logistics, Services, Supplies and other Duties essentially to the above mentioned Industries.

The Directors of TTC Ltd. had previously worked separately as single entities and/or Directors of other Companies in Hospitality, Transport and Supplies industries and have a rich experience of over 10 years.

Where You Can Locate Us

We are currently located in Nairobi, Kenya the business hub of Eastern and Central Africa, with a sub-branch in Mombasa, Kenya (the main Sea port).
